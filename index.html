Andre Felipe Zanella

Hello, I'm a 2nd year Telematics Engineering PhD Student @ IMDEA Networks Intitute and UC3M in Madrid, Spain.

About me

  • I'm a part of the Networks Data Science Group, under the supervision of Dr. Marco Fiore. There, I work with statistical analysis and data mining of the metadata that flows under mobile networks. I'm mostly interested in remove sensing and statistical modeling of network behaviors through the lenses of operational networks.

  • I'm currently based in Cambridge as a visiting student at the Computer Lab of the University of Cambridge. I've previously been located at the SENSE Lab for Orange Innovations in Paris, France.

  • Before starting my PhD, I received both my Master and Bachelor degrees in Telecommunications Engineering from UFPR in Curitiba, Brazil.
