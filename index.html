Andre F. Zanella

Researcher and Networks Engineer

Hello!

I’m Andre, a Researcher, Engineer and Data Scientist making a fruit salad out of these themes. I mainly enjoys transforming internet and network measurements into intestering tools and insights.

I’m currently a Research Scientist at Telefónica Innovación Digital, where my focus is on creating tools that transform large-scale network measurements into useful insights for both the MNO, as well as parties interested in the cool stuff you can do with mobile networks data.

Research and education

I received my PhD in Telematics Engineering from UC3M, and my Masters and Bachelors in Electrical Engineering from UFPR.

Throughout my PhD, I worked as a Research Assistent at IMDEA Networks (quite the cool place, highly recommend) at the Networks Data Science Group, under the supervision of Dr. Marco Fiore.

You can check my publications in my Google Scholar.

Personal

My main hobby is listening to music and pretending I can make it. Sometimes I also complain about movies. In the past few years, to justify that my life is not bound to a computer screen, I’ve been hiking and climbing a bit, as well as getting back into electronics projects (to justify the dust on my EE diploma).
